Pizza
Pizza is rich in carbohydrates, protein, and fat.
Whole wheat crust provides more fiber than refined flour.
Limit extra cheese to reduce saturated fat.
Pair pizza with a fresh salad for a balanced meal.

Burger
Burgers provide protein but can also be high in saturated fat and sodium.
Choose grilled patties and whole wheat buns for a healthier option.
Add vegetables like lettuce and tomato.

French Fries
French fries are high in calories and fat due to deep frying.
Eating them occasionally and in small portions is recommended.

Salad
Salads are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber.
Adding lean protein like grilled chicken improves nutrition.

Banana
Bananas are rich in potassium and natural carbohydrates.
They are a great pre-workout snack.

Apple
Apples are high in fiber and vitamin C.
Eating apples with the skin provides maximum nutrition.

Ice Cream
Ice cream is high in sugar and saturated fat.
Enjoy it occasionally and in moderate portions.
